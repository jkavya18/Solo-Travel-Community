Group Travelling

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Activities: White-water rafting, bungee jumping, camping, and trekking. Ideal for: Adventure-seeking friends or corporate teams.

Manali & Solang Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Activities: Paragliding, skiing (in winter), and trekking. Ideal for: Groups of friends who love snow or mountains.

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Activities: Bike trips, Nubra Valley sand dunes, Pangong Lake camping. Ideal for: Adventure-loving groups looking for road trips.

Meghalaya

Activities: Trekking to living root bridges, caving, and exploring waterfalls. Ideal for: Nature-loving groups seeking offbeat adventures.

Gokarna, Karnataka

Activities: Beach hopping, camping, and yoga retreats. Ideal for: Relaxed, budget-friendly group outings.

Pondicherry, Tamil Nadu

Activities: Beachside cafes, cycling tours, and Auroville exploration. Ideal for: Groups seeking a tranquil, French-inspired retreat.

Munnar, Kerala

Activities: Tea plantation walks, Eravikulam National Park, and boating. Ideal for: Groups seeking a peaceful getaway in lush greenery.

Kaziranga National Park, Assam

Activities: Elephant safaris and spotting one-horned rhinoceroses. Ideal for: Wildlife photographers and nature-loving groups.
